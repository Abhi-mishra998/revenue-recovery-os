## Problem (≤ 300 chars)

```
Indian SMB founders run revenue from a spreadsheet. Deals get forgotten,
follow-ups go unsent, invoices quietly slip overdue. The spreadsheet is
the leak. They don't need another CRM to learn — they need their existing
file to tell them where money is hiding.
```

## Solution (≤ 500 chars)

```
Upload your CSV/Excel. Within 3 seconds Revora shows what's in your
file — customers, pipeline, overdue invoices — by heuristic, before any
AI runs. AI then maps your columns with confidence chips, you confirm,
and you land on a Revenue Health report: visibility score, three
actions to take today, ₹ at risk, ₹ recoverable if you act today.
Every number cites its evidence. Every AI output shows its confidence.
Every action is ed25519-signed. Founder approves every send.
```
